What our metadata cleaner removes

Every metadata standard used by cameras, phones, and AI generators: EXIF, GPS, XMP, IPTC, C2PA, and more.

  • EXIF
    Camera & Device Data
    Camera model, lens, ISO, aperture, shutter speed, software version, device serial numbers, and timestamps.
  • GPS
    Location & Geotags
    GPS coordinates, altitude, and embedded location data added automatically by phones and modern cameras.
  • XMP
    Editing History
    Adobe XMP metadata including full editing history, software used, colour profile, and workflow data.
  • IPTC
    Author & Copyright
    Creator name, email, copyright, keywords, captions, and categorisation data from the IPTC standard.
  • C2PA
    Content Credentials
    Adobe's provenance standard that triggers "Made with AI" labels on Instagram, Facebook, and Pinterest.
  • PNG Chunks
    Stable Diffusion Settings
    Generation parameters embedded in PNG files by Automatic1111, ComfyUI, and Forge: prompts, seed, CFG scale, sampler, and model hash.
  • AI Signatures
    Generator Fingerprints
    Embedded signatures from DALL-E, MidJourney, Adobe Firefly, and other AI image tools.
  • Hash
    Digital Fingerprint
    Invisible shifts to individual pixel values change the file's unique fingerprint, disrupting reverse-image search and duplicate detection.

How to remove metadata from images

Four steps, all running locally in your browser. Nothing is sent to a server, ever.

01
File stays on your device
Your image is read directly into browser memory. Nothing is sent over the internet at any step.
02
All metadata removed
The image is redrawn from raw pixels and saved as a fresh file. All embedded metadata is discarded in the process.
03
Fingerprint randomized
A handful of pixels are shifted by 1–2 color values, invisible to the eye but enough to change the file's unique fingerprint and break reverse-image search.
04
Clean download
Your clean image is ready to save. After you download it, all image data is deleted from your browser automatically.

What this tool can't do

What CleanMetaDATA does

  • Removes embedded metadata: EXIF camera data, GPS location, timestamps, XMP and IPTC tags
  • Strips AI generation markers including C2PA Content Credentials and AI software tags
  • Alters the image's perceptual fingerprint to disrupt reverse-image search and duplicate matching
  • Runs entirely in your browser. Images are never uploaded to or stored on any server.

What it can't do

  • Cannot remove invisible watermarks such as Google's SynthID. Those are baked into pixels, not metadata.
  • Cannot change how a platform's visual AI detection analyzes the image itself
  • Cannot guarantee a specific result on Pinterest, Instagram, or any platform. Detection algorithms update without notice.
  • Cannot undo a label a platform has already applied to a previously uploaded image
